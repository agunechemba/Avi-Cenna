Student Life

Beyond the Classroom

At Avi-Cenna, education is about more than just textbooks. We provide a rich tapestry of experiences that allow students to explore their passions, develop leadership skills, and build lifelong friendships.

Sports & Athletics

From swimming to football, our students compete at high levels, learning teamwork and resilience.

Creative Arts

Our drama, music, and art departments encourage students to express their unique creativity.

Clubs & Societies

Including STEM club, Debate society, and the Red Cross, there is a community for every interest.

School Culture

Our "House System" fosters healthy competition and a sense of belonging. Weekly assemblies and annual cultural days celebrate the diverse backgrounds of our international community.
